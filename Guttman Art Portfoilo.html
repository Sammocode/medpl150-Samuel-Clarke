What is Art

Art is the expression or application of human creativity, and to me, it is that emotion of freedom that takes the mind out of politics or drama unless it's a narrative story, including exploring adventure in other media such as books, movies, cartoons, video games, etc. It takes skill and imagination, typically in a visual form, to appreciate primarily for their beauty or emotional power.
