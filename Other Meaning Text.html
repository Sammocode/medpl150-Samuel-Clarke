What have you learned additionally about each man through his own words? Why is it important that you tell your own story outside of stereotypes and popular media?

Malcolm X, the Man and anyone own words towards Media

Some mythology can frame them oppositely, both can influence each other, and face challenges, including racism. It is very important to tell your own story because you know the story and the truth, like in independent publishing, but the mainstream media and stereotypes can be very dangerous, especially when lies, bias, and hypocrisy can portray you in a bad light, and you know the truth and portray it better than anyone else to be accurate.
