Creative Statement

As a university student majoring in Media Studies with a concentration in Emerging Media at Hunter College, I am focused on developing skills in game development, storytelling, animation, and interactive design. My academic path includes a Film Minor for writing and a Business Studies Certificate, which helps me better navigate the media industry.

I also hold an Associate Degree (AAS) in Information Technology from Guttman Community College, where I studied web development (HTML/CSS), Java IDE Programming, and Networking. I am interested in developing digital media that explores emotional resonance while offering new modes of engagement, especially in character-driven environments.

My current focus is on creating original IPs that combine animation, narrative structure, and gameplay mechanics. I plan to apply these skills toward building original projects and studio ventures. This statement defines my academic direction and creative goals for the semester.
